# Non-goals (Early Milestones)

The following are explicitly out of scope for M00 and nearby milestones:

- No sketch constraint solver.
- No FEA/CFD solver.
- No unified PDE engine.
- No early fillet/chamfer feature work.
- No browser WASM kernel target in early phases.
- No up-front monolithic "all-in-one CAD app" architecture.

These are deferred to keep early milestones focused on kernel foundations and disciplined iteration.
